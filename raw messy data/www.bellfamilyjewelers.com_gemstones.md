# Gemstone Education

Before making a purchase knowing the salient features of your gemstone is essential. At Bell Family Jewelers we can help you!

# Gemstone Color

A gemstone is judged to be of the highest quality by its spotlessness of the hue, the intensity of the tone and color saturation. A gemstone would receive a high value if it is neither too light nor to dark and also has a few traces of colors in it.

# Gemstone Cut

A gemstone is judged to be of the highest quality by its spotlessness of the hue, the intensity of the tone and color saturation. A gemstone would receive a high value if it is neither too light nor to dark and also has a few traces of colors in it.

# Gemstone Enhancements

Heat-treatment is given to almost all colored gemstones and the process is called enhancement. Gemstones which are enhancement free and still have the color and clarity are extremely expensive.

# Gemstone Clarity

Most of the gemstones contain inclusions, which are made by tiny fractures. Even the most high quality gemstone has slight inclusions as flawless gemstones are a rarity and very expensive.

# Gemstone Size

The actual measure of the gemstone size cannot be evaluated by its carat eight. When a gemstone is set on jewelers, only the above surface can be viewed.

# Gemstone Care

It is important that you take care of your precious gemstones so that they can become heirlooms.
